About

Check out this mini website project I’ve crafted using HTML5 and CSS, adhering to industry best practices. Flexbox really adds to the responsiveness of the page. This project is my way of sharpening my web design and development skills before diving into larger portfolio pieces.

After nearly two years away due to long-term disability, I'm excited to re-enter the workforce and showcase my resilience and growth. I'm a fighter, and disability is just a hurdle I've overcome. I'm actively seeking a remote frontend engineering role. My current focus is on enhancing my expertise in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and Angular.

When I'm not immersed in tech, my heart belongs to my fiancé Sheena, our rat Squirrel, and our cat Princess. Web design and development, however, remains my greatest passion. Since taking a summer Apple HyperCard class on a Mac SE30 back in the '80s, I’ve been hooked on all things computer-related. While I've explored various IT fields during my time on disability, web development has always been a constant. I’ve rediscovered my love for modern HTML, CSS, and Angular, and hold several web certifications from CIW, along with the AWS Solutions Architect certification. I’m also Comptia A+ and Network+ certified.
